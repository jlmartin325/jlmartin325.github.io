Elkhart-Goshen, IN Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Elkhart-Goshen Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 126,529 in 1970 to 197,561 in 2010. That was an increase of 56.1 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 2,288 in 1970 to 15,403 in 2010. That was an increase of 573.2 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 1.8 percent to 7.8 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 46.6 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
