Wheeling, WV-OH Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Wheeling Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ decreased from 182,712 in 1970 to 147,950 in 2010. That was a decrease of 19.0 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population decreased from 5,013 in 1970 to 1,508 in 2010. That was a decrease of 69.9 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 2.7 percent to 1.0 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) decreased by 17.6 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
