Bend-Redmond, OR Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Bend-Redmond Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 30,442 in 1970 to 157,733 in 2010. That was an increase of 418.1 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 531 in 1970 to 6,741 in 2010. That was an increase of 1169.5 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 1.7 percent to 4.3 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 404.8 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
