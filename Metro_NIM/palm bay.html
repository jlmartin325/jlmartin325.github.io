Palm Bay-Melbourne-Titusville, FL Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Palm Bay-Melbourne-Titusville Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 230,006 in 1970 to 543,376 in 2010. That was an increase of 136.2 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 6,429 in 1970 to 47,182 in 2010. That was an increase of 601.4 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 2.8 percent to 8.7 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 121.9 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
