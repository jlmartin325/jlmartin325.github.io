Kalamazoo-Portage, MI Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Kalamazoo-Portage Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 257,723 in 1970 to 326,589 in 2010. That was an increase of 26.7 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 7,816 in 1970 to 14,262 in 2010. That was an increase of 82.5 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 3.0 percent to 4.4 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 25.0 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
