Omaha-Council Bluffs, NE-IA Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Omaha-Council Bluffs Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 815,502 in 1970 to 1,202,832 in 2010. That was an increase of 47.5 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 18,893 in 1970 to 83,289 in 2010. That was an increase of 340.8 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 2.3 percent to 6.9 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 40.9 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
