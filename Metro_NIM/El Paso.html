El Paso, TX Metro Area Immigration

Population

The El Paso, TX Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 361,683 in 1970 to 804,088 in 2010. That was an increase of 122.3 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 48,121 in 1970 to 211,686 in 2010. That was an increase of 339.9 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 13.3 percent to 26.3 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 88.9 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
