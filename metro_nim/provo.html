Provo-Orem, UT Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Provo-Orem Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 142,350 in 1970 to 526,810 in 2010. That was an increase of 270.1 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 2,994 in 1970 to 37,072 in 2010. That was an increase of 1159.2 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 2.1 percent to 7.0 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 251.3 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
