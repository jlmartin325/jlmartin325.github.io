Crestview-Fort Walton Beach-Destin, FL Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Crestview-Fort Walton Beach-Destin Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 104,274 in 1970 to 235,265 in 2010. That was an increase of 125.6 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 1,750 in 1970 to 16,548 in 2010. That was an increase of 845.6 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 1.7 percent to 7.0 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 113.3 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
