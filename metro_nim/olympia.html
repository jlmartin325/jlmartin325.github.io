Olympia-Tumwater, WA Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Olympia-Tumwater Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 76,894 in 1970 to 252,264 in 2010. That was an increase of 228.1 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 2,197 in 1970 to 17,115 in 2010. That was an increase of 679.0 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 2.9 percent to 6.8 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 214.8 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
