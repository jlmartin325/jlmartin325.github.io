Pueblo, CO Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Pueblo Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 118,238 in 1970 to 159,063 in 2010. That was an increase of 34.5 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 2,652 in 1970 to 5,884 in 2010. That was an increase of 121.9 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 2.2 percent to 3.7 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 32.5 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residueal "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
