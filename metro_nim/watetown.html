Watertown-Fort Drum, NY Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Watertown-Fort Drum Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 88,508 in 1970 to 117,011 in 2010. That was an increase of 31.7 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 3,277 in 1970 to 5,971 in 2010. That was an increase of 82.2 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 3.7 percent to 5.1 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) decreased by 29.8 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
