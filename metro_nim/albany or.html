Albany, OR Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Albany, OR Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 103,069 in 20000 to 117,648 in 2013. That was an increase of 14.1 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 3,591 in 2000 to 5,184 in 2013. That was an increase of 44.4 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 3.5 percent to 4.4 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 13.1 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
