San Luis Obispo-Paso Robles-Arroyo Grande, CA Metro Area Immigration

Population

The San Luis Obispo-Paso Robles-Arroyo Grande Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 105,690 in 1970 to 269,637 in 2010. That was an increase of 155.1 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 5,319 in 1970 to 29,935 in 2010. That was an increase of 462.8 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 5.0 percent to 11.1 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 138.8 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
