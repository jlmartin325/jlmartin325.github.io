Pocatello, ID Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Pocatello Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ increased from 52,200 in 1970 to 83,071 in 2010. That was an increase of 59.1 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 819 in 1970 to 1,773 in 2010. That was an inecrease of 116.5 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 1.6 percent to 2.1 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) increased by 58.2 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.

The second chart shows the same data but with an adjustment to reflect births to immigrants shifted to NIM. In it, B-D remained the major factor adding population in all three periods.
