Waterloo-Cedar Falls, IA Metro Area Immigration

Population

The Waterloo-Cedar Falls Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) population 1/ decreased from 169,772 in 1970 to 167,819 in 2010. That was a decrease of 1.2 percent.

The foreign-born (FB) population increased from 2,279 in 1970 to 6,684 in 2010. That was an increase of 193.3 percent. The foreign-born share changed from 1.3 percent to 4.0 percent.

The share of the overall population that was native-born (NB) decreased by 3.8 percent.

Sources of Population Change

The primary sources of population change are:
  • Natural Change [Births minus Deaths or B-D];
  • Net Domestic Migration [NDM - movement between states]; and
  • Net International Migration [NIM - movement into and out of the country].
  • There is also a residual "other" amount that isn't placed in one of those three categories.
